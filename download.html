AWS Certification

Validate technical skills and cloud expertise to grow your career and business.

AWS Certification Account Access
Prepare for an exam

AWS Certification validates cloud expertise to help professionals highlight in-demand skills and organizations build effective, innovative teams for cloud initiatives using AWS. Choose from diverse certification exams by role and specialty designed to empower individuals and teams to meet their unique goals.

Explore our role-based certifications for those in Cloud Practitioner, Architect, Developer, and Operations roles, as well as our Specialty certifications in specific technical areas.

The Latest in AWS Certification

Share Your Achievement with Enhanced Digital Badges

AWS Certification provides digital badges as a benefit to showcase your certification status. By making the transition to provide digital badges via Credly’s Acclaim platform, we now offer more flexible options for recognition and verification. Take advantage of one-click sharing on social media newsfeeds, tools for embedding verifiable badges on websites, and an optional public profile with all earned AWS Certification badges.

 

Learn more

Free Webinars on AWS Certification Exam Prep

Preparing for your next AWS Certification? Join one of our free, live webinars led by AWS-accredited instructors. A complement to our free Exam Readiness digital training courses, these webinars can help prepare you for AWS Certification by exploring main exam topics and identifying specific areas to study. Ask questions via chat during the Q&A, and feel confident on exam day.

 

Learn more

AWS Certification Exams Now Available Virtually

You can now take all AWS Certification exams from the comfort of your home with online proctoring. With this added convenience and flexibility, it’s now easier than ever to show your cloud skills are backed by an AWS Certification. For more information on this option, as well as other ways we are supporting candidates who need more time to reschedule exams, recertify, and use vouchers, visit our FAQ page.

Learn more

AWS Certified Data Analytics - Specialty Certification

Validate your expertise in AWS data lakes and analytics services from collection to visualization. Earn the AWS Certified Data Analytics - Specialty certification and demonstrate experience in designing, building, securing, and maintaining analytics solutions on AWS that are efficient, cost-effective, and secure. This certification was previously named AWS Certified Big Data - Specialty.
